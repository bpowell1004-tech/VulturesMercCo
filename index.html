History & Legacy

In the shadow of a once‑mighty order known only as the Crusade, a new brotherhood rose from the ashes. The Vultures trace their origins to this enigmatic precursor – a force that guarded technology and humanity in equal measure. When the Crusade's era ended, a seasoned warrior named Brad Maximus Hawthorne forged a smaller, more agile company dedicated to preserving what remained of their ideals. Today, the Vultures operate independently, offering their skills to protect convoys, gather intelligence and rescue those in need, always mindful of the past that shaped them.

Recruitment

Become part of the Vultures. Applicants must be 18 years or older, possess a working microphone, complete our basic training, and agree to follow the company's guidelines. Pay comes from contracts you accept – Brad receives 20% of mission profits and the remainder is divided among participating members.

Ranks & Roles

  • Recruit: New members who must accompany a Talon on missions.
  • Talon: Junior mercenaries trusted with basic missions and support roles.
  • Vulture: Experienced mercenaries qualified for most missions.
  • Beak Commander: Officers who lead squads and handle tactical decisions.
  • Patriarch: Overall leader; chooses which missions to accept but cannot be hired directly.
